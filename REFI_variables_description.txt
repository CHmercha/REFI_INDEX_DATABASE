*company_CAP : market capitalization of each company

*company_FI : the initial weight assigned to each company

*company_min : the minimum weight allowed for each company (set at 5%)

*sum_of_weights : the sum of all minimum weights

*REMAIN : the remaining total weight that will be redistributed according to the adjusted weighting of individual companies

*company_adjusted : the adjusted weights

*INDEX_Value_in_EU : the new REFI index capitalization (in euros)

*company_number : the number of shares for each company

*company_ajust_number : the number of shares for each company after the dividend adjustment

*price : the price of one REFI share
